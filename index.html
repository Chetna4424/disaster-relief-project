About the Project

The Flexible Disaster Relief Allocation System is a smart and efficient model designed to optimize the delivery of essential resources during disasters.

It uses graph algorithms (Dijkstra’s Algorithm) to identify the shortest and fastest routes from relief centers to affected areas, while a priority queue mechanism ensures that high-urgency zones receive immediate attention.

The system aims to make disaster response faster, data-driven, and reliable, reducing delays and maximizing the impact of relief operations.
