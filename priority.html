Concept Overview

To determine the order of delivering resources to affected areas, a Priority Queue is used. It ranks disaster zones based on urgency and distance from the relief center.

  • Urgency Levels: 1 = High, 2 = Medium, 3 = Low
  • Distance: Used as a secondary factor if urgencies are equal.

Sorting Approach

  1. Collect all disaster areas with urgency and distance.
  2. Sort first by urgency (ascending order).
  3. If urgency is the same, sort by distance.
  4. Deliver supplies in the sorted order.

Example

AreaUrgencyDistance (km)Delivery Order
Area BHigh (1)61
Area AMedium (2)42
Area DMedium (2)103
Area CLow (3)84

This ensures critical areas receive relief first while minimizing total travel time.
